Welcome to My Creative Universe!

Hey there! I'm Rodrigo, but you can call me Ro or Drigo. I'm a fervent creator with a knack for building websites, crafting code, and expressing myself through various art forms. I firmly believe in the mantra that anyone can achieve anything with the power of confidence.


Currently, I'm dedicating my time to two fascinating roles. Firstly, I'm a proud volunteer at the Video Games and Esports Foundation, an organization dedicated to supporting schools financially and fostering a pathway for students. Within this role, I engage with the vibrant streamer community, striving to bring essential financial backing to our cause. Secondly, I'm deeply involved as a tennis coach at Lakeland Academy of Tennis. Tennis has been a part of my life since the tender age of 7, and now I'm channeling my extensive knowledge and passion for the sport to mentor kids and teenagers, guiding them towards their aspirations in high school or college.


My journey has been nothing short of intriguing. Originating from a background in Law School at one of Brazil's premier institutions, I felt the call for change. This led me to delve into the world of IT, where I embarked on a self-taught journey mastering Python. Subsequently, I pursued formal education at Anhembi Morumbi in Brazil, where I honed my skills further. But my appetite for challenge persisted, propelling me towards newer horizons.


Passion, ambition, and resilience are at the core of who I am. I approach every endeavor with unwavering determination, ensuring that I see it through to completion. My adeptness at forging connections and my prowess as a team player make me a valuable asset in any collaborative environment.


I draw immense inspiration from the legendary Roger Federer, not just as a tennis icon, but as a symbol of resilience and continual self-improvement. His ethos of never giving up and consistently surpassing one's limits resonates deeply with me. My grand vision is to merge my passion for tennis with software development, embarking on a groundbreaking project that intertwines these seemingly disparate worlds.


Let's Connect!

Looking forward to our future endeavors together!
